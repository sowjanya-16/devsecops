Featured Food
Scrumptious food is where it's at with our newest recipes, egg bites and delectable appetizers. Plus desserts too!

ACTIVE ENERGY BAR BITES
At Active Eats, guests will be able to find this new sweet, which will leave them full of energy! The Active Eats Energy Bar Bites are a small and light treat made from the mixture of chocolate, nuts, dried fruit, and dates.

CHICKEN ENCHILADA SOUP
Everything you love about chicken enchiladas… in a bowl! Added with some black beans, corn and chicken broth, thrown into the crock pot. Tasty, filling with just the right amount of kick!

JERK SPICED CHICKEN LOLLIPOP
A dish that is not only savory but sweet is the Jerk-spiced Chicken Lollipop, made with roasted sweet plantain salad and a mango chutney yogurt. This offering can be found at popular North sites.

CHOCALATE PICANTE
A toothsome offering that packs a little spice, coming to the chini's, is the Chocolate Picante. This rich offering is a dark chocolate mousse with cayenne pepper, chili powder, and mango.

MAPLE BOURBON CHEESECAKE
The Maple Bourbon Boursin Cheesecake, made with maple bourbon cream, caramel and pecans is a sweet delight with a little crunch.

DAHI VADA
This North Indian delicacy has evolved as the favorite snack of many Indians. The deep fried vada is laced with a generous helping of curd, topped with tamarind or coriander chutney. A sprinkle of black salt or chaat masala adds to the tangy sweet taste of Dahi Vada.
